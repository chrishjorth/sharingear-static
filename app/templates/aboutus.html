About Us

Sharingear is the first trusted community marketplace for musicians and touring personnel to find the best and cheapest deals when it comes to concerts and touring. Musicians have the opportunity to list gear, rent gear and even find the right sound-engineer. Technicians have the opportunity to showcase their profiles, and bands can find the right type of transportation in order to lower productions costs. Through personnalized profiles users can add and manage their instrument rentals or bus/van orders, and connect with the right type of technicians In other words Sharingear is the ultimate marketplace for touring musicians.
