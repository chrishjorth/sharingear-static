Sharingear is built on relationships. Help others to get to know you.

Tell them about your achievements, and in what part of the industry you come from. If you are a musician, tell them about the tours you have done, your favourite instruments and how many records you have recorded.

If you are a technician, tell them about how many tours you have done, what bands have you worked with and what you would like to work more on.

If you have a van or a bus, tell them about which bands you have been on the road with, and what other tours or bands you would like to work with.

Tell them about you.
