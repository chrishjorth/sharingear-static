Our mission is to change
how artists tour!

Our vision is to ease the life of musicians through a sharing economy platform. We want musicians to share their instruments, and thereby play more, save costs, and make money while home.

With the rise of music streaming services, the sales in the physical medium is dropping by 5-10 % each year. Profitable music sales has become a daunting task, and therefore many artists are forced to hit the road, and play more live shows than ever before.

This is not a definite bad thing, the more live shows you play, the more exposure you get. But, a big challenge for touring bands is managing the logistics of being on the road. At Sharingear we want to ease the life of the touring musician, and thereby direct the musician’s focus on the performance, rather than on the hard work backstage. With the aid of Sharingear.com, musicians have access to a peer-2-peer platform that enables a sharing economy between bands, artists and venues. Thus, your band has the opportunity to create a favorable situation by teaming up with musicians in other cities or countries. For example, renting the backline from a local band, enables you to leave all of your heavy gear at home, and travel solely with your string-instruments. The instrument renter saves cost, and the instrument owner increases revenue, and thereby both parties harness the benefits that arise when using the Sharingear platform.
