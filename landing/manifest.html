Our mission is to change
how artists tour!

It is becoming harder and harder to sell music, and artists need to play live shows in order to reach their audiences. The sales in the physical medium are dropping by 5-10% each year, and fans have been driven to the “all you can eat” model by the streaming services. Artists are now forced to hit the road harder than ever before.

The hardest part for a touring band are the transportation costs or rental for equipment.

Through Sharingear your band has the opportunity to connect with bands in other towns. With Sharingear you are able to lower these transportation costs by teaming up with other bands in other cities or countries. By renting the backline from a local band, you can travel with your string-instruments and leave all of your heavy cabs & amps at home and save a lot of money.

If you want to grow your reach, create band awareness, and build a steady following, you need to play more live shows. Sharingear helps bands do what they do best, play live!
