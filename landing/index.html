Play more live shows

Sharingear is a peer-to-peer marketplace that lets you rent instruments and equipment from other musicians.

How? - Here is an example:

2 bands can team up and rent a van, drive to the venue and rent backline locally by connecting with a local band. The local band can make money by renting out the gear to the bands coming from out of town. The bands from out of town save the transportation costs, and dont have to push heavy equipment around.

  • Share your instrument (or backline)
  • Avoid breaking your back
  • No more pushing heavy gear
  • Save costs
  • Make money
  • Play more!

Concerned? Sharingear insures all your instruments!
