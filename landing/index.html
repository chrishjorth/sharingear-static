Sharingear is a peer-to-peer marketplace that lets you rent instruments and equipment from other musicians.

How? - Here is one example:

2 bands can team up and rent a van, drive to the venue and rent backline locally by connecting with a local band. The local band can make money by renting out the gear to the bands coming from out of town. The bands from out of town save the transportation costs, and don't have to push heavy equipment around.

  • Share your instrument (or backline)
  • Avoid breaking your back on tour
  • Save costs
  • Make money
  • Play more!

Concerned? Sharingear insures all your instruments!
