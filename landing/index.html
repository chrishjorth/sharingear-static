Sharingear is a peer-to-peer marketplace that lets musicians rent out their instruments and equipment to other musicians. Check this out:

  • Want comfort and a better tour or show experience?
  • Want to save transportation costs? Then rent all the heavy equipment you already are pushing around at each and every show.
  • How? Here is an example: 2 bands can now rent a van, drive to the venue and rent backline locally and connect with other bands.
  • The local band can make money by renting out the gear in the rehearsal.
